# DM Script Library

## Principles
*   **No "Hey hun"**: Be professional, direct, and masculine/neutral.
*   **No Walls of Text**: 2-3 sentences max.
*   **Context is King**: Reference something specific (their DJ, their renovation, their last weekend).

## Objection Handling

**"We have a guy."**
> "Cool. Is he delivering next-day? We're not trying to replace him, we're an engine for volume. Use us for the nights he can't make, or for fast-turnaround reels."

**"Too expensive."**
> "It's $345. That's two bottle service tabs. One good reel pays for itself in cover charge alone."

**"Send me a PDF."**
> "Happy to. Check [gettupp.com](#) for the full breakdown. But honestly, the best proof is the Pilot. Let's just do one night."

# GettUpp ENT - Brand Identity

## Core Identity
*   **Name**: GettUpp ENT
*   **Tagline**: "We Own Your Feed."
*   **Mission**: Deliver predictable, AI-enhanced nightlife content that drives venue revenue through speed, consistency, and measurable results.
*   **Voice**: Direct, Energetic, ROI-oriented, Nightlife-savvy.

## Visual Language
*   **Aesthetic**: Dark mode, neon accents, high contrast, grain is okay if it adds vibe.
*   **Color Palette (Canonical)**:
    *   **Ink (Background)**: `#0B0B0D`
    *   **Gold (Primary)**: `#D9AE43`
    *   **Pink (Accent)**: `#FF3C93`
    *   **White**: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
*   **Headings**: Poppins or Montserrat (Bold, Condensed, Uppercase).
*   **Body**: Inter or Roboto (Clean, High legibility).

## Content Rules
1.  **No Boring Crowds**: If they aren't screaming, dancing, or toasting, delete it.
2.  **Hero the DJ**: The DJ is the captain. Make them look god-tier.
3.  **Speed Kills**: Delivery within 24-72h is our moat.

## Brand Assets
*   **Wordmark**: Gold/Pink Leather (`wordmark_gold_pink`)
*   **Monogram**: GE Gold/Pink (`monogram_GE`)
*   **Founder Persona**: Johnny Cage (Nightlife Creator/Expert)
